About Us

Our Community in Kenya faces the challenge of waste management and dumpsite proximity.Due to the unhygienic environment, people are exposed to diseases such as Cholera, Asthma, Diarrhea, Dengue Fever, Food poisoning, and stomach flu.

Cholera in the year 2023, killed more than 4300 people globally. It has 1.3 to 4.0 million cases of infection annually across the world.

Asthma has accounted for over 300 million cases of infection annually across the world. An additional 100 million people are projected to be at risk of infection.

Diarrhea is the third leading cause of death in children 1- 59 months of age. 443,832 children under 5 years die annually from diarrhea globally. Each year, 1.7 billion cases of childhood diarrhea are reported globally. It is the leading cause of malnutrition in children under the age of 5 years. 1200 kids die daily globally.

Dengue Fever has surged from 500,000 cases to more than 4.2 million cases in only 2 decades globally.

Stomach flu accounts for 200 million child infection cases and 50,000 deaths annually.

Food poisoning has over 600 million cases yearly with 420,000 deaths reported annually.
